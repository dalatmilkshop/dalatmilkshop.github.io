Comparing Pasteurized & UHT Dalatmilk for Kids

Updated: May 19, 2024 | By Dalatmilk Shop Da Nang

What is Pasteurized Milk?

Pasteurized milk is heated to a moderate temperature for a short time to kill harmful bacteria while preserving most nutrients and flavor. It must be kept refrigerated and has a shorter shelf life.

What is UHT Milk?

UHT (Ultra-High Temperature) milk is heated to a higher temperature for a few seconds, making it shelf-stable for months without refrigeration until opened. Some nutrients and flavor may be reduced compared to pasteurized milk.

Which is Better for Kids?

  • Nutrition: Pasteurized milk retains more natural nutrients and taste.
  • Convenience: UHT milk is easier to store and transport, ideal for busy families.
  • Safety: Both are safe, but always check the expiration date and storage instructions.

Tips for Da Nang Families

  • Choose pasteurized Dalatmilk for daily use if you have a fridge and want the freshest taste.
  • Keep UHT Dalatmilk on hand for travel or emergencies.
  • Buy from trusted distributors for guaranteed quality.
