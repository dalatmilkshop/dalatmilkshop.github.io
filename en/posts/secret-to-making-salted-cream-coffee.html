Secret to Making Salted Cream Coffee with Dalatmilk

Updated: May 20, 2024 | By Dalatmilk Shop Da Nang

Why Salted Cream Coffee?

This trendy drink combines the bitterness of coffee with the rich, creamy, and slightly salty foam, creating a unique flavor that attracts customers to your café.

Ingredients

  • 60ml strong brewed coffee (espresso or phin)
  • 80ml Dalatmilk fresh milk
  • 30ml whipping cream
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 10g sugar
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Whip the cream, Dalatmilk, salt, and sugar together until thick and foamy.
  2. Fill a glass with ice, pour in the coffee, then gently top with the salted cream foam.
  3. Serve immediately and enjoy the perfect balance of flavors!

Barista Tips

  • Use Dalatmilk for the freshest, creamiest foam.
  • Adjust salt and sugar to taste for your customers.
  • Offer as a signature drink to stand out in Da Nang’s café scene.
