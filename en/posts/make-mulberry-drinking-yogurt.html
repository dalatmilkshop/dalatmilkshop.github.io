Make Your Own Mulberry Drinking Yogurt Like a Pro

Updated: May 18, 2024 | By Dalatmilk Shop Da Nang

Ingredients

  • 1 bottle Dalatmilk drinking yogurt
  • 50g fresh mulberries (or mulberry jam)
  • 1 tbsp honey or sugar (optional)
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Mash the mulberries with honey or sugar until juicy.
  2. Fill a glass with ice, pour in the Dalatmilk yogurt, and add the mulberry mixture.
  3. Stir well and enjoy a refreshing, healthy drink!

Tips

  • Use fresh Da Nang mulberries for the best flavor.
  • Adjust sweetness to taste.
  • Great for kids and adults alike during hot summer days.
